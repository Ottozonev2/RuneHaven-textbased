World of Eryndor
The Veil between worlds is broken. Violet lightning tears the sky. The dead walk freely and entire kingdoms have warped into unnatural wastelands. You are an Outworlder — a soul dragged through the Veil during the failed Shattered Summoning — cast into the dying world of Eryndor with no memory of how or why.

Two great factions war for the soul of this world. The Luminous Covenant would reopen the Veil and restore the gods. The Ashen Dominion worships the Devouring King — the entity that slipped through during the summoning.

Choose your allegiance. Shape the fate of Eryndor.
