The Veil between worlds is broken. Amber lightning tears the sky. The dead walk freely and entire kingdoms have warped into unnatural waste-lands. Two great factions war for the soul of this world. The Luminous Covenant would reopen the Veil and restore the gods. The Ashen Dominion worships the Devouring King — the entity that slipped through during the summoning.

Choose your allegiance. Shape the fate of Eryndor.
